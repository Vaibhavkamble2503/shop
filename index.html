About Our Store

Welcome to दुर्गा भेळ, Welcome to Durga Bhel, where every meal is a journey to the heart of authentic flavours. Nestled in the heart of the city, our cafe is dedicated to serving the finest Bhel and Pani Puri that will transport you to the bustling streets of India.

At Durga Bhel, we believe in the power of fresh ingredients, traditional recipes and a touch of love to create food that brings people together. Whether you want the spiciness of Pani Puri or the perfect blend of spices in our Bhel, we ensure that each dish is made with the utmost care and passion.

Our story began with a simple dream - to share the joy of India's beloved street food in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Over time, this dream has grown into a place where friends and families come together to celebrate great food, laughter and unforgettable memories. Join us at Durga Bhel and experience the magic of authentic Indian street food, prepared just the way you like it. We look forward to serving you!
